## AI-Assisted Financial Insights

This section documents insights generated with AI support to explain financial trends identified through SQL and Power BI analysis.

### Key Insights
- Identified high-spend categories contributing most to overall expenses.
- Detected monthly trends useful for budget planning and forecasting.
- Highlighted anomalies that may indicate data quality issues or unusual transactions.

### Business Value
AI-assisted analysis helped translate raw financial metrics into clear business insights, supporting data-driven decision-making for financial stakeholders.

### Note
AI tools were used to assist with insight generation and business interpretation, while data analysis and validation were performed manually.
