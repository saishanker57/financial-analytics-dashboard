## AI-Assisted Financial Insights

This section documents insights generated with AI support to interpret trends identified through SQL-based financial analysis.

### Key Insights
- Sales consistently generated higher revenue and profit compared to Marketing across all analyzed months.
- Both departments showed steady month-over-month revenue growth, indicating improving operational performance.
- Marketing expenses consumed a higher percentage of revenue compared to Sales, highlighting potential opportunities for cost optimization.
- Overall profitability increased each month, driven primarily by revenue growth rather than expense reduction.

### Business Value
AI-assisted analysis helped summarize financial trends, highlight cost efficiency differences between departments, and translate numerical outputs into clear insights for budget planning and management review.

### Note
AI tools were used to assist with insight interpretation and business explanation. All data preparation, validation, and SQL analysis were performed manually.
